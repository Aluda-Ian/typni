Empowering the Next Generation: Inside Typni’s Youth-Centered Events

  • Admin
  • 15 Nov, 2024

At The Young People's Network International (Typni), we believe in the power of youth to shape the future. Our events are designed to equip young people with the skills, knowledge, and connections they need to thrive in today’s fast-changing world. Here’s a look at three key events shaping the future of Kenya’s youth.

🔐 High-Level Cybersecurity Youth Session: Securing the Digital Future

With the rise of cyber threats, digital security is no longer optional—it's a necessity. The High-Level Cybersecurity Youth Session brings together industry experts and tech-savvy youth to discuss cybersecurity trends, best practices, and career opportunities in the field.

  • Learn from cybersecurity professionals.
  • Understand the latest threats and solutions.
  • Discover career opportunities in cybersecurity.

🚀 YES Summit: Unleashing Entrepreneurial Potential

The Youth Entrepreneurship Summit (YES Summit) is a platform where young entrepreneurs connect, learn, and grow. With mentorship, funding insights, and business workshops, this summit is designed to empower the next generation of business leaders.

  • Expert-led entrepreneurship workshops.
  • Networking with investors and successful entrepreneurs.
  • Strategies for scaling startups in Kenya and beyond.

🌟 ACSAP-K Gamechangers Career Event: Shaping Future Leaders

The job market is evolving rapidly, and young professionals need to stay ahead. The ACSAP-K Gamechangers Career Event provides youth with career guidance, industry insights, and mentorship opportunities.

  • One-on-one mentorship sessions.
  • Career coaching and CV reviews.
  • Networking with top employers and industry experts.

"The youth are the heartbeat of our future. By investing in their growth, we build a stronger, more innovative society."

— Charlene Ruto, Founder of Typni

At Typni, we are committed to empowering young minds and creating opportunities for growth. Be part of the change—attend our events, engage with like-minded individuals, and take the next step toward your future!

📅 Stay updated on upcoming events by following our social media channels and visiting our website. 🚀 *The future belongs to the bold. Are you ready to step up?*
